Questions

Before you ask

Why per business instead of per employee?

Charging per seat means a manager hesitates before inviting the whole team. Once you're on a plan with team notes, every staff member can use it without adding to the bill one by one.

Do customers or employees ever pay?

No — nobody submitting or backing a note ever pays, on any plan. You're only ever paying, as the business, for the dashboard and features — and on Starter, that doesn't yet include the private team notes channel.

What happens if I go over on Starter?

There's no note or vote limit to hit. Starter is capped by feature depth (no team notes, no Trends), not by usage — so a busy business never gets throttled.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes — move from Starter to Growth whenever your team's ready for a private feedback channel too. Nothing about your existing customer notes changes.
