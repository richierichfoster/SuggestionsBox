7. Moderation

Notes are checked, using a combination of automated tools and — where flagged — human review, against section 6 before being posted. Depending on what's found:

  • Content that clearly breaches section 6 (e.g. a personal attack or profanity) is blocked from posting, and the author is offered a chance to rewrite it.
  • Content that's borderline — critical of a named individual but not clearly abusive, for instance — may be blocked from public posting but offered to the Business privately by email instead, so genuine feedback isn't lost even where it isn't suitable to publish.

Automated moderation isn't perfect. We don't guarantee that every Note that should be blocked will be, or that every Note that's blocked deserved to be — if you believe a Note was moderated in error, contact us (see section 15).
